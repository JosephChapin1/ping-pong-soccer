Why Play Ping Pong Soccer?

Fast-Paced Game

The game requires constant motion and quick decision making.

Quick to Learn

Players with experience playing Ultimate Frisbee, Basketball, Soccer, or Lacrosse will find the rules familiar and quick to master.

Highly Competitive

While quick to pick up, the game takes many hours to master and supports a number of different play styles.
